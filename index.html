Win/Lose/Try Again msg

Key

  • White: Open Peg
  • Options: Red, Black, Gold, Peach, Maroon, and Mint Green.
  • Yellow highlight: Color is included in answer.
  • Green highlight: Color is correct and in the right sequence.
How to play MasterMind:

A "secret" code is chosen at the beginning of each game the you must figure out! To do so you must:

  1. Click on the bottom row's empty pegs to cycle through your color options.
  2. Once all pegs are chosen in the first line, click "Enter".
  3. Your answer will be checked against the secret one and it will tell you per "peg" which ones are either included but not in the right sequence (yellow) or that are both correct in color and sequence (green).
  4. If your answer is not fully correct, the next row above will open for you to make your selections.
  5. You have six guesses total, so make 'em count!
  6. Note: It is possible to have multiples of the same color in the code!
  7. Note: If you guess a color that has already been highlighted green, it is still possible that if you guess that color elsewhwere, it will highlight yellow subsequently even though there may only be one.
